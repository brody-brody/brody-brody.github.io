About the Project

Tasked to make a game using the verbs decrease and evade in just 4 weeks, our team chose to create a retro 2D bullet hell with our own unique movement mechanics, shrinking and growing. Inspired by some internet culture including alien cats and small dogs, we crafted this game. You play as the alien cat, Gnarpznap, fighting off the big boss, Eeble Geeble against an invasion threatening the world.

Features

  • Shrinking and growing movement mechanics to both evade and absorb bullets
  • Boss transition animations
  • Easily expandable architecture for future growth

Development Process

Most of the development process went very smoothly on this project, as our goals were scoped well within what we thought we could reach. My favorite element of development was the easily expandable bullet pattern system using scriptable objects. An abstract base class, bullet pattern, allowed me to derive multiple types of patterns with easy expandability, and then a separate scriptable object, bullet pattern group, allowed the chaining of patterns at different intervals and weights. A designer could open the editor and create an entire set of boss attack patterns, including different pattern pools for different phases, all without writing a single line of code.
